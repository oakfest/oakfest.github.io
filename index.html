About

PRESENTED BY WHITEHURST, BLACKBURN & WARREN, ATTORNEYS AT LAW.
The Kiwanis Club of Thomasville invites you to make a difference in the life of a child by supporting our annual event and fundraiser, OakFest, which will be held on Saturday, March 16, 2019 from 3:30 p.m.-10:00 p.m at the Ritz Amphitheater in Downtown Thomasville.

Our event is a full day of music, food, and fun! We will have bands from across the southeast, featuring Hiss Golden Messenger w/ Neighbor Lady, local food trucks, plus a beer and wine garden, for all your dining needs.

As members of the Kiwanis Club, our goal is to serve the children of our community in the best ways possible: literacy, scholarships, and financial support to organizations like the Treehouse to promote healthy and positive assistance to children.
